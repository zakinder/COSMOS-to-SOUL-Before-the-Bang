## Overview

*COSMOS to SOUL: Before the Bang* is a metaphysical and engineering-inspired book architecture that connects digital verification concepts with spiritual accountability, unseen trials, moral testing, and the journey of the soul.

The work uses the language of **UVM**, **DUTs**, **testbenches**, **agents**, **monitors**, **scoreboards**, **coverage closure**, **fault injection**, **regression testing**, **final sign-off**, and **waveform replay** to explain a deeper spiritual idea:

> Every human life is under test.  
> Every action is logged.  
> Every intention has weight.  
> Every soul moves toward final acknowledgment.

This repository organizes the book into a structured design framework so the reader can understand the relationship between engineering verification and the soul’s journey from worldly life to eternal accountability.
