# 7. Cover Design Direction

## Visual Theme

The cover should combine:

- Deep cosmic background
- Pre-creation darkness
- Emerging light
- A single central point or dot
- Spiral motion
- Golden record symbolism
- Subtle circuit or waveform lines
- Soul-like luminous center
- Cosmic horizon
- Sacred geometry
- Verification-grid structure

## Suggested Colors

| Color | Meaning |
|---|---|
| Deep black | Before creation, void, stillness |
| Dark blue | Cosmos, depth, mystery |
| Gold | Truth, record, divine value |
| White | Light, clarity, revelation |
| Purple | Spiritual depth, unseen reality |
| Silver | Verification, precision, engineering structure |
