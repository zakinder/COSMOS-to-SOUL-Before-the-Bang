## Purpose

The **Spiritual Scoreboard** defines how the soul’s responses are observed, compared, recorded, corrected, and finalized within the *COSMOS to SOUL* verification framework.

In hardware verification, a **scoreboard** compares expected behavior against actual behavior. It receives observed transactions from monitors, checks them against a reference model, and reports mismatches.

In this spiritual framework:

- **The soul** is the Design Under Test.
- **Life events** are stimulus.
- **Actions, words, intentions, and choices** are output transactions.
- **Conscience, angels, witnesses, and divine knowledge** act as monitors.
- **Truth, moral law, revelation, and divine justice** act as the reference model.
- **The Book of Deeds** functions as the final immutable scoreboard.
- **Repentance and mercy** provide correction before final sign-off.
- **Death** closes the update window.
- **Judgment Day** replays and audits the complete scoreboard.

The scoreboard does not merely count actions. It evaluates intention, context, repetition, correction, sincerity, and final state.

# 1. Scoreboard Definition

## Spiritual Scoreboard

A **Spiritual Scoreboard** is the complete moral-verification record of a soul.

It tracks:

- Stimulus received
- Response produced
- Intention behind response
- Moral expectation
- Actual outcome
- Witness confirmation
- Error or pass status
- Repentance or recovery status
- Regression status
- Coverage impact
- Final judgment relevance

The scoreboard is not public popularity, social reputation, or human opinion. It is the true record preserved under divine knowledge.

# 14. Final Sign-Off Behavior

At death, the scoreboard update window closes.

Before death:

- New good deeds can be added.
- Failures can be corrected.
- Debts can be repaired.
- Apologies can be made.
- Repentance can transform the record.
- Regression can prove growth.
- Mercy can be sought.

After death:

- The record is sealed.
- No new worldly deeds are added.
- No further direct correction is performed.
- The scoreboard awaits final audit.
- Judgment Day reveals the full trace.

```txt
Life Active  ->  Scoreboard Writable
Death        ->  Scoreboard Sealed
Judgment     ->  Scoreboard Replayed
Verdict      ->  Scoreboard Finalized
Eternity     ->  State Locked
```
