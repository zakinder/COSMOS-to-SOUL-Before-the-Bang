## Purpose

This document expands the **Golden Sequences and Test Cases Framework** into a dedicated testcase library.

Each testcase translates a spiritual or moral situation into a verification-style structure using concepts such as:

- **DUT** — Design Under Test / the soul
- **Stimulus** — life event, trial, temptation, hardship, or opportunity
- **Monitor** — conscience, angels, witnesses, memory, body, or divine knowledge
- **Checker** — moral law, truth, scripture, conscience, or divine specification
- **Scoreboard** — the Book of Deeds / final record
- **Coverage** — virtues exercised through life events
- **Regression** — repeated trials that confirm stable growth
- **Fault Injection** — adversity, temptation, betrayal, or pressure
- **ECC / Recovery** — repentance, mercy, correction, and restoration
- **Sign-Off** — death and sealed record
- **Waveform Replay** — Judgment Day review
- **Deployment** — eternal outcome

This library is designed to support:

- **Chapter 26 — Testcases & Sequences of the Soul**

## Testcase Format

Each testcase follows this structure:

| Field | Meaning |
|---|---|
| **Testcase ID** | Unique identifier for the spiritual verification scenario |
| **Name** | Short descriptive testcase name |
| **Golden Sequence Link** | Related golden sequence phase |
| **Verification Concept** | Engineering verification idea being mapped |
| **Scenario** | Human-life or afterlife situation |
| **Stimulus** | Event or condition applied to the soul |
| **Expected Response** | Desired moral/spiritual behavior |
| **Monitors** | Observers that capture the response |
| **Checker / Scoreboard** | Standard used to judge the response |
| **Pass Criteria** | Conditions for successful testcase result |
| **Fail Criteria** | Conditions for testcase failure |
| **Recovery Path** | Repentance, correction, apology, or restoration |
| **Coverage Target** | Virtue or moral quality exercised |

# Conclusion

The **Testcase Library of the Soul** converts the spiritual verification framework into a structured set of practical scenarios. It allows the reader to see how life events, trials, temptations, repentance, death, judgment, and eternal outcome can be understood through verification discipline.

The purpose is not to reduce the soul to machinery. The purpose is to use engineering precision as a language for understanding accountability, moral growth, divine monitoring, and final truth.

In this framework:

- Life is the testbench.
- The soul is the DUT.
- Trials are stimulus.
- Angels are monitors.
- Conscience is an internal checker.
- Repentance is error correction.
- Death is sign-off.
- Judgment is waveform replay.
- The afterlife is eternal deployment.
- The final record is immutable truth.
