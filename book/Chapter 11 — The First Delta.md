# 1. The End of Absolute Stillness

For an immeasurable state beyond time, the Void remained unchanged.

No sound disturbed it.
No motion crossed it.
No light illuminated it.

Everything existed in perfect suspension.

The silence was complete.

The stillness was absolute.

The symmetry appeared eternal.

Yet beneath the frozen perfection of nonexistence, hidden instability waited unknowingly for release.

And then—

the impossible happened.

A movement appeared.

Not large.

Not violent.

Almost nothing.

Yet inside Absolute Nothingness, even the smallest movement becomes infinite in consequence.

This movement became known as:

> The First Delta.

---

# 2. What Is a Delta?

In ordinary language, a delta represents change.

A deviation.

A shift between one condition and another.

But before the universe, no conditions had previously changed.

The First Delta therefore became:

* The first variation,
* The first asymmetry,
* The first interruption in perfect stillness.

It was not merely movement.

It was the birth of movement itself.

The Void had never encountered difference before.

And because it had never encountered difference, it could not survive it.

* No physics.

A shift occurred where no shift should have been possible.

This contradiction became the first mystery of all creation.

The universe begins not with certainty—

but with impossibility.
