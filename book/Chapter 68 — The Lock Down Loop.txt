# 2. What Is the Lock Down Loop?

The Lock Down Loop is:

A loop forms when:

* Fear repeats,
* Trauma repeats,
* Conflict repeats,
* Illusion repeats,
* Identity freezes.

Motion still exists—
but growth stops.

The soul continues moving internally while remaining confined.

The universe reveals:

> Not all motion produces freedom.

Creation can become recursively trapped.
