## Purpose

The **Immutable Golden Record Index** defines the permanent archive layer of the *COSMOS to SOUL* spiritual verification framework.

In engineering verification, a **golden record** is the trusted reference, baseline, or preserved source of truth used to compare expected behavior against observed behavior. Once finalized, it must not be corrupted, overwritten, or altered.

In the *COSMOS to SOUL* framework, the Immutable Golden Record represents the complete, preserved, and incorruptible record of the soul’s journey.

It includes:

- The original divine specification
- The soul’s life trace
- All observed actions
- All spoken words
- All hidden intentions
- All tests encountered
- All failures
- All corrections
- All repeated patterns
- All witnesses
- All coverage results
- All final judgments
- All eternal outcomes

This document acts as the index layer that connects the book’s golden sequences, testcase library, coverage map, scoreboard, and regression suite into one permanent record architecture.
